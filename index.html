My Story

My name is Meghan Ryan and I am an elementary art teacher in Schaumburg, IL. I enjoy all types of art but am newly getting into Photography and Ceramics. I just finished up my Masters in Curriculum and Instruction with an ELL endorsement from National Louis University. Art has always had a way of bringing me back down to earth and letting me explore new ideas. I am thankful and lucky that I get to create everyday and inspire others to do the same.

I like to stay busy! Previously I have interned at the Rockford Art Museum and the Museum of Contemporary Art in Chicago. Art History was my minor and I try and incorporate it into my teaching as much as possible.
